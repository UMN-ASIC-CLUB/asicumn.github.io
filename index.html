// About

Most students never touch real silicon. Our members ship it.

We exist to close the gap between coursework and the fab. From your first Verilog module to a fabricated die in your hand, the club gives you the tools, the cluster, and the teammates to get there.

⌁

Learn the full flow

RTL → synthesis → place-and-route → GDSII, taught hands-on through our RTL-to-GDS curriculum.

▣

Tape out real chips

We target open-source PDKs like SKY130 and Tiny Tapeout shuttles — designs that actually get fabricated.

⚙

Industry-grade tools

Cadence, Synopsys, and Siemens EDA flows running on our own cluster — the same tools used in industry.

◈

Build a portfolio

Microarchitecture, verification, and physical design — the concrete skills that semiconductor teams hire for.

// The Flow

Every project moves through the same pipeline. You can plug in at any stage — front-end design, verification, or back-end physical implementation.
